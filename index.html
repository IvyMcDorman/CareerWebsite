The perks and benefits of being an:

Aquarium Animal Curator

Job Responsibilities

Aquarium animal curators have several responsibilities, including:

  • deciding what fish to put in the Aquarium
  • regulating the nutrition and health of the fish
  • determining the required water conditions
  • determining the types of glass tanks needed

Job Description

An aquarium curator oversees one specific group of animals in an
aquarium's animal collection such as fish or mammals.
